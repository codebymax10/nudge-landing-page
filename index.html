Will my clients know it's automated?

No. Nudge sends emails from your own address using natural, personal language. Your clients will think you wrote it yourself — because it sounds exactly like a professional, considerate email from you.

What invoice tools does Nudge work with?

You can forward any invoice email to Nudge and it parses the details automatically. We're also building direct integrations with FreshBooks, Wave, and Notion — more coming soon.

Can I customise what the emails say?

Yes. On the Pro and Studio plans you can edit the tone, adjust specific wording, and even write fully custom templates. Nudge just fills in the client name, invoice amount, and due date.

What if a client pays before a reminder goes out?

Mark the invoice as paid and Nudge cancels all pending reminders instantly. You can also set Nudge to detect payment confirmations automatically if you forward payment receipts to the same inbox.

Is it really free during beta?

Yes — full Pro features, free, for everyone on the waitlist during the beta period. When we launch publicly, beta users lock in a discounted rate for life.
